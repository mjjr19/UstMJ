Ustadh Mohamad Joban's Book

"Kiprah Dakwah Imam Mohammad Joban" highlights Ustadh Mohamad Joban's journey preaching Islam in the United States. It includes inspiring stories of people converting to Islam, his experiences as a prison chaplain, and the transformation of many prisoners to Islam.

This book offers valuable insights into the challenges and rewards of spreading Islam in a diverse society.
